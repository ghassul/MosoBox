More for the Fans

At MosoBox, we dedicate our time to working with artists and game developers to create quality merchandise of their works. To us, each product is a new way for these creators to get in touch with their fans.

How it's done

All the grit of production planning, distribution, and financing are handled by us. This is so our partners can focus on the design and spreading the news of their new products.

Production

We handle all the fine negotiation and production details. The findings from these are then delivered to you in a concise summary outlining the important details and options available. Our supplier network is filled with both local and international manufacturers which we have been building relationships with over several years. With our experience and network connections we are confident in being able to find the best mix of product quality and pricing.

Distribution

Everything needed for shipping we already have. This with being based in Vancouver, BC, Canada we can be quick in shipping to fans across North America. In addition to this we attend conventions in our area as a vendor. Like this, we are able to sell merchandise at these events on behalf of our partners increasing the amount of exposure and sales.

Financing

Our goal is to provide a supplemental income for our partners at little to no risk. We offer no cost merchandising as we use the finances from our previous ventures and convention sales to cover these costs. Depending on the nature of the merchandising deal we can offer a variety of other benefits. Benefits such as forwards on royalties and additional promotional merchandise, all at no costs to our partners.

About

MosoBox is a merchandise dealer and vendor based in Vancouver BC, Canada. Started as an artist alley table in 2014 we then expanded operations to work with more artists and creators to have their works turn to merchandise to better reach fans and markets.
