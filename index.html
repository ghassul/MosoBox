Who we are

MosoBox is a merchandise dealer and vendor based in Vancouver BC, Canada. MosoBox started as an artist table in 2014 before we expanded our operations to work with more artists and creators to turn their works into merchandise to better reach fans and markets abroad.

Merchandise Dealer

We are used to dealing with many manufacturers having worked with a variety of both local and international artists for our inventories at conventions. With this experience and network we are confident in being able to find the right mix of quality and pricing for items being made.

Convention Vendor

We attend conventions both within our local area of Vancouver as well as abroad as we venture out to other conventions. If allowed we will sell the items at these events on behalf of the creator(s) or artist(s) which increases the amount of exposure for those involved as well as sales.

Putting Quality First

We want to make sure that the products we are selling are what the creator or artist would be happy with, so when possible we ship samples of said products to them to make sure it is up to their standards.

A Little Bit of Everything

With our variety of suppliers and extensive background with vendoring we built a varied supply network which allows us to mix and match a variety of items together to make varied bundles for online sale purposes.
