Who we are

MosoBox is a merchandise dealer and vendor based in Vancouver BC, Canada. Started as an artist table in 2014 we then expanded operations to work with more artists and creators to have their works turn to merchandise to better reach fans and markets

Merchandise Dealer

We are use to dealing with manufactures having work with a variety of both local and international ones for our inventories at conventions. With this experience and network we are confident in being able to find the right mix of quality and pricing for items being made

Convention Vendor

We attend conventions both within our local area of Vancouver as well as venture out to other conventions in other not so far off areas. If allowed we will sell the items at these events on behalf of the creator or artist which increase the amount of exposure and sales

Putting Quality First

We want to make sure that the products we are selling are what the creator or artist would be happy with, so when possible we ship samples of said product to them to make sure it's up to their standards

A Little Bit of Everything

With our variety of suppliers and background with vendoring we built a varied supply network which allows us to mix and match a variety of items together to make varied bundles for online sale purpose
